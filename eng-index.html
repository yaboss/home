We connect you

Yaboss was originally created by a group of expatriates in Bali as a solution for business owners to seek reliable employees in their particular fields, however, this soon extended from the workplace to everyday lives. Yaboss goes beyond your standard 9-5 job search, opening up a whole new world of opportunities right at your fingertips. Whether you’re looking for a one time job, freelance work, part time or full time employment, Yaboss keeps you connected with all of the latest job updates in your area..

Hire a worker

Having trouble finding qualified experts in your field?
Maybe you are after a freelancer for a one time job?
From part time and full time employment to photographers,
babysitters, gardeners and more, Yaboss is here to help.

All you need to do is post your job request to Yaboss.

We will notify all of the qualified candidates in your area.

Applicants are prompted to apply for the desired role.

You are free to select suitable applicants for the position

Find a job

Stop searching the endless web for employment!
Yaboss is here to keep you connected with relevant employers
and job opportunities in your area at the touch of a button.

All you need is a smartphone, a quick sign up to our app and we will take care of the rest.

Every time an employer posts a new job with your credentials you will be notified.

You are free to accept or deny the job offer.

Once you have accepted the offer, is it then up to the employer to choose the best one for the position
